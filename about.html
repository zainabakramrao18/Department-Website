Department Introduction

Centre of Advanced Studies in Pure and Applied Mathematics (CASPAM) is dedicated to excellence in teaching and research in pure and applied mathematics. The department fosters analytical thinking, innovation, and academic growth, preparing students for advanced research and diverse professional careers.

Vision

    To become a leading center of excellence in pure and applied mathematics, recognized nationally and internationally for impactful research and high-quality education. To cultivate critical thinking, innovation, and mathematical expertise that contribute to scientific advancement and societal development.

Mission

  • Provide quality education.
  • Encourage research and innovation.
  • Develop leadership and teamwork skills.

Message from Chairperson

"Our goal is to prepare students for the challenges of the modern digital world through quality education and research opportunities."
