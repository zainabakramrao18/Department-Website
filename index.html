Welcome to Our Department

Centre of Advanced Studies in Pure and Applied Mathematics (CASPAM) provides quality education in pure mathematics, applied mathematics, statistics, and advanced computational methods.

CASPAM is committed to academic excellence, innovative research, and the intellectual and professional development of students, preparing them for careers in academia, research, industry, and data science.
